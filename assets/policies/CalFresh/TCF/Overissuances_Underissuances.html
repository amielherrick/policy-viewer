Overissuances/Underissuances

During the TCF period, the household’s TCF benefit allotment is frozen. Overissuances and underissuances are assessed on Transitional CalFresh cases ONLY when the case is set up for

Transitional CalFresh incorrectly due to worker error. The Transitional CalFresh budget is NOT adjusted to correct for errors that occurred prior to the Transitional CalFresh period.

ExampleExample

The client is discontinued effective 03/31/18 due to exceeding the income limit. The case is set up for Transitional CalFresh for 04/18-08/18. During the Transitional CalFresh set-up, the EW discovers an error in the shelter deduction allowed for 06/17 through 03/18. The rent of $350 was erroneously entered in the system as $650. Transitional CalFresh budgeting will include the shelter deduction of $650 which was allowed in the 03/18 CalFresh budget even though this was an incorrect amount. The CalFresh budgets for the months prior to Transitional CalFresh (06/17 through 03/18) would be revised and an overissuance established, if appropriate, for these months.

Note: When rescinding CalWORKs, if good cause is granted and it is too late to stop the issuance of Transitional CalFresh or Transitional CalFresh benefits have already been issued, there is no overissuance for the month Transitional CalFresh was issued.

 

Households restoring CalWORKs and CalFresh benefits within 30 days of the CalWORKs discontinuance do not need to submit a new CalFresh application. In cases where the CalWORKs discontinuance is rescinded, there is no overissuance because TCF benefits must be calculated based on available information at the time.
