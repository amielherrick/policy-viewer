Ineligible Students

[63-406.3, 63-505.65]

Any student (refer to Applicability [63-406.1 & 2] ) who does not meet the Student Eligibility Criteria is ineligible for CalFresh and considered a non-household member. Refer to Ineligible Student Excluded Members for information on the treatment of an ineligible student's income and resources.

Note: Student ineligibility is determined based on when the student enrolls/registers for higher education.
